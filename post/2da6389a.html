Synchronous Double-channel Recurrent Network for Aspect-Opinion Pair Extraction [Link] [Code]

College of Artiﬁcial Intelligence, Nankai University, Tianjin, China

ACL 2020

Introduction

Opinion Entity Extraction:

  • Aim to identify aspects and/or opinion expressions

  • Considerable studies focus on the related tasks:

    • Aspect term extraction
    • Opinion term extraction
    • Aspect and opinion term co-extraction

  • Recent studies neglect to recognize the relations between aspects and opinion expressions.

    • Both “nice-looking” and “delicious” express positive sentiment
    • “nice-looking”: appearance perspective
    • “delicious”: taste perspective
    • Only with the relations between aspects and opinion expressions, can the more fine-grained subsequent tasks be executed
      • Pair-level sentiment classification

Aspect-Opinion Pair Extraction (AOPE)

  • Studies on AOPE are very limited
  • Three challenges:
    • How to effectively detect relations.
      • Relational structure can be complicated.
      • The relations can be one-to-many, many-to-one, and even embedded or overlapped.
    • How to properly fuse opinion entity extraction and relation detection.
      • The two subtasks rely on each other.
    • How to synchronize opinion entity extraction with relation detection and make them mutually promotion.
