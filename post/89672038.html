Position-Aware Tagging for Aspect Sentiment Triplet Extraction
2021-07-15|NLP
字数总计: 845|阅读时长: 4 分钟

Position-Aware Tagging for Aspect Sentiment Triplet Extraction [paper] [code]

Lu Xu, Hao Li, Wei Lu, Lidong Bing
StatNLP Research Group, Singapore University of Technology and Design
DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group
ByteDance

EMNLP 2020

Background

Aspect Sentiment Triplet Extraction (ASTE):

  • A relatively new task proposed by Peng et al. (2019) in AAAI-2020.

  • Definition: ASTE is the task of extracting the triplets of target entities, their associated sentiment, and opinion spans explaining the reason for the sentiment.

Motivation

Existing research efforts mostly solve this problem using pipeline approaches:

  • Extract target entities and opinion spans at 1st1^{st}1st stage,
  • Pair the target and opinion at the 2nd2^{nd}2nd stage.

which break the triplet extraction process into several stages.

However, the three elements in a triplet are highly related to each other, which implies that jointly capturing the rich interaction among three elements in a triplet might be a more effective approach.

In this paper:

We propose the first end-to-end approach, JET, to Jointly Extract the Triplets based on our position-aware tagging scheme.

Approach

Position-aware tagging scheme

We extend the tag B and tag S in the BIOES tagging scheme to new tags respectively.

where ϵ∈{+,0,−}\epsilon \in \{+,0,-\}ϵ∈{+,0,−} denotes the sentiment polarity, and jjj and kkk indicate the position of opinion span.

Input: Food was so so but excited to see many vegan options

Output: [(‘food’, ‘so so’, ‘neutral’), (‘vegan options’, ‘excited’, ‘positive’)]

BIOES 标注方法的两个缺陷：

  1. BIOES tagging scheme without encoding any positional information fails to specify the connection between a target and its opinion span as well as the rich interactions among the three elements due to the limited expressiveness. 没有位置信息，三元组中的元素缺少交互。

  2. Using such a tagging scheme inevitably leads to an additional step to connect each target with an opinion span as the second stage in the pipeline approach. 需要pipeline结构的第二阶段去匹配 target 与 opinion span 。

Position-aware 标注方法可以解决 opinion-target 一对多的特殊情况，如 “The salad is cheap with fresh salmon”，其中有两个 triplet ：(“salad”, “cheap with fresh salmon”, positive) and (“salmon”, “fresh”, positive)，“fresh” 在两个 triplet 中的 opinion span 重叠了，而 (Peng et al., 2019) 的pipeline结构则无法处理该情况（opinion重叠情况，Peng et al., 2019 只能抽取出 “fresh” 或 “cheap with fresh salmon”）。

One Target for Multiple Opinion Spans

Peng et al. (2019) 的pipeline方法是构造 opinion-aspect 一对一的pair，而上述方法可以解决 opinion-aspect 一对多的情况，那么如果存在 aspect-opinion 一对多的情况，如何解决？只需要将 target 与 opinion span 的标注方法进行交换。所以作者又提出了JET的变体：

  • JETtJET^{t}JETt 表示解决 opinion-aspect 一对多的模型
  • JEToJET^{o}JETo 表示解决 aspect-opinion 一对多的模型

在实验中，作者做了Ensemble Analysis，就是将 JETtJET^{t}JETt 与 JEToJET^{o}JETo 两个模型的结果合并作为最终结果。
