A Position-aware Bidirectional Attention Network for Aspect-level Sentiment Analysis

COLING 2018 [paper]

Shuqin Gu 1 , Lipeng Zhang 2 , Yuexian Hou 1∗ and Yin Song 1

1 School of Computer Science and Technology, Tianjin University, Tianjin, China

2 School of Computer Software, Tianjin University, Tianjin, China

Introduction

现有的工作都是通过一系列的注意力机制来建模 aspect term 和 sentence 的关系，然而他们忽视了位置的信息，与 aspect term 相邻的词应该比远距离的词要受到更多的关注。

因此，本文提出了 position-aware bidirectional attention network (PBAN) 。

Model consists of three components:

  1. Obtaining position information of each word in corresponding sentence based on the current aspect term, then converting the position information into position embedding.
  2. The PBAN composes of two Bi-GRU networks focusing on extracting the aspect-level features and sentence-level features respectively.
  3. Using the bidirectional attention mechanism to model the mutual relation between aspect term and its corresponding sentence.

Main contributions:

(1) We attempt to explicitly investigate the effectiveness of the position information of aspect term for aspect-level sentiment analysis.

(2) We propose a position-aware bidirectional attention network (PBAN) based on Bi-GRU, which has been proved to be effective to improve the sentiment analysis performance.

(3) We apply a bidirectional attention mechanism, which can enhance the mutual relation between the aspect term and its corresponding sentence, and prevent the irrelevant words from getting more attention.

Position-aware Bidirectional Attention Network Model (PBAN)

Attention model in PBAN consists of two parts: including the aspect term to the position-aware sentence part and a position-aware sentence to the aspect term part.

对于前者，我们可以根据 aspect term 中的不同单词获得句子的不同隐藏上下文表示。

For the former part, we can obtain the different hidden contextual representation of a sentence according to different word in aspect term.

对于后者，我们可以根据位置信息获得 aspect term 中词的注意力权重，用于获得句子的最终表示。

For the later part, we can obtain the attention weights of the words in aspect term according to the position information, which is used for getting the ﬁnal representation of a sentence.

Experiments Setting

Parameters Setting:

Word embedding: 300-dimension Glove.

The number of the hidden units are set to 200.

The dimension of position embedding is set to 100, which is randomly initialized and updated during the training process.

Dataset:

The SemEval 2014 dataset consists of reviews in Restaurant and Laptop datasets.
