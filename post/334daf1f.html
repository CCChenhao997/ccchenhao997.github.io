Context-aware Embedding for Targeted Aspect-based Sentiment Analysis

ACL 2019 [paper]

Bin Liang 1 , Jiachen Du 1 , Ruifeng Xu 1∗ , Binyang Li 2 , Hejiao Huang 1

1 Department of Computer Science, Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen), China

2 School of Information Science and Technology, University of International Relations, Beijing, China

Introduction

这篇论文的任务是 TABSA 任务，和 ABSA 任务还有有点区别，可以理解成 ABSA 任务是 TBSA 任务的子任务。

The objective of TABSA task is to detect the aspect a∈Aa ∈ Aa∈A and classify the sentiment polarity p∈{Positive,Negative,None}p ∈ \{Positive, Negative, None\}p∈{Positive,Negative,None} according to a speciﬁc target ttt and the sentence sss.

现在的很多方法都没有为 targets 和 aspects 特别地预训练合理的 embeddings 。这可能会导致 targets 或 aspects 在不同的上下文中却有着相同的向量表示，这会丢失上下文依赖信息。

为了解决这个问题，作者提出了一种新的方法去 refine the embeddings of targets and aspects。具体地，我们使用一个稀疏稀疏向量 (a sparse coefﬁcient vector) 从句子中选择高度相关的词，然后调节 the embeddings of targets and aspects。因此，targets 和 aspects 的 embeddings 就可以被从高度相关的词被 refined，而不是直接使用上下文独立或者随机初始化的向量。

Contributions：

  • We reconstruct the vector representation for target from the context by means of a sparse coefﬁcient vector, hence the representation of target is generated from highly correlative words rather than using context-independent or randomly initialized embedding.
  • The aspect embedding is ﬁne-tuned to be close to the highly correlated target and be away from the irrelevant targets.
  • Experiment results on SentiHood and Semeval 2015 show that our proposed method can be directly incorporated into embedding-based TABSA models and achieve state-of-the-art performance.
