DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group

Singapore University of Technology and Design

AAAI 2020

Introduction

本文是第一个提出 aspect sentiment triplet extraction (ASTE) 任务.

本文提出了一个两阶段的框架用于解决ASTE任务。

  • For the first stage, we aim to extract potential aspect terms, together with their sentiment, and extract potential opinion terms.

    Aspect-sentiment 使用 (Li et al. 2019a) 提出的 unified tagging 方案，opinion 使用 BIO-like tagging。

  • For the second stage, the goal is to pair up aspects with the corresponding opinion expressions.

    利用位置信息来帮助 aspect 与 opinion 的配对。

Model Overview

Stage One

Uniﬁed aspect boundary and sentiment labeling.

这部分与 Li et al. (2019a) 的工作基本类似。

Opinion term extraction.

引入了GCN与BiLSTM，相当于结合了句法信息和语义信息。GCN的邻接矩阵是通过依赖解析得到。

Stage Two

After stage one, for each sentence, we output two sets of text segments, i.e., aspect terms and opinion expressions, denoted as {T1,T2,...,Tn}\{T_1, T_2, ..., T_n \}{T1​,T2​,...,Tn​} and {O1,O2,...,Om}\{O_1, O_2 , ..., O_m \}{O1​,O2​,...,Om​} respectively, where there are n aspects and m opinion expressions.

Then, we generate a candidate pair pool as {(T1,O1),(T1,O2),...,(Tn,Om)}\{ (T_1 , O_1 ), (T_1 , O_2 ), ..., (T_n , O_m ) \}{(T1​,O1​),(T1​,O2​),...,(Tn​,Om​)} by enumerating all possible aspect-opinion pairs. Stage two is to classify whether each of these pair is valid or not.

Position embeddings.

Pair encoder and classiﬁcation.

将位置embedding与 Glove word embeddings 拼接输入到 BiLSTM ，然后将 aspect 和 opinion 位置单独做 average，再将它们拼接，输入到 softmax 层做分类，详细见模型图。
