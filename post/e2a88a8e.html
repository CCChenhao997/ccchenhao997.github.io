各阶段准备

开场：

  • 稍正式些

    It’s great to be here with you today. 很高兴今天能来这里

    Thanks for having me today. 感谢邀请我

  • 稍随意

    Hi everyone, good morning. 直接问候早上下午好之类的

    Hi guys, how’s everyone doing?

 
介绍自己：

稍正式：

Let me briefly introduce myself. 我先简短的介绍一下自己

My name is (chenhao) and I’m the (student) of (BUPT). 我叫某某，我是某单位的某职位

 
今天要说啥:

Today, I’d like to talk to you about …

My topic today is …

 
Overview - 今天概述

Let me direct your attention to the screen. 请大家看大屏幕

(As you can see) This presentation is divided into (four) parts. 今天的展示主要分四部分

 
详细概述

I want to begin by/with (按情况选介词) A,

and then I’ll move on to B,

next we’re talking about C,

and finally we’ll land on D (maybe a conclusion).

 
具体内容

Let me begin by giving you some background information about (this issue).

先给大家介绍一些背景信息吧

To get started, let’s take a look at (this chart) here.

首先咱们先看这个表

 
之后就说自己的第一部分

 
下一个话题（第二部分）

Now, I’d like to move on to the second part of the presentation.

Moving on to the next topic.

To give you an example, let’s take a look at this. 举例子

A good example of this is …

 
结尾

That concludes my presentation.

That brings us/brought us to the end of my presentation.

 
Q&A Time

Does anyone has any questions or comments ? 大家有问题或有什么要说的吗？

 
Pass the baton 把接力棒给下一人

If there are no further questions at this point, I’d like to give the stage to my coworker/teammate Amy.

如果没什么问题了呢，有请我的同事/队友Amy来为大家做下面的讲解。

 
如果没有同事/队友了

That’s it from me, thank you for your attention. 我的就结束了，感谢大家。

 

内容

  1. 根据Presentation的主题简单罗列需要传达的内容，如内容模块A/内容模块B/内容模块C/…
  2. 按照顺序重新排列组合：开头+简介—>内容模块A—>内容模块B—>内容模块C—>(…)—>总结+结尾
  3. 详细展开每一个内容模块。内容模块A—>A1—>A2—>A3，内容模块B—>B1—>B2—>B3，内容模块C—>C1—>C2—>C3
  4. 修改内容及语句+完善过渡语，例如：首先/其次/再次/(…) 让表达更有逻辑
  5. 一边练习一边完善

 

Slides Requires:

  • Structure

  • Outline

  • Members

  • Keywords

  • Resonable
